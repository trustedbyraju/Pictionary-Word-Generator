❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about the Pictionary Word Generator.

What is the Pictionary Word Generator?
▾
The Pictionary Word Generator is a free online tool that generates random words for playing Pictionary. Whether you're hosting a family game night, a party, or a classroom activity, simply choose a difficulty category and tap generate to get a word instantly — no sign-up required.
Is this tool completely free to use?
▾
Yes, 100% free — forever. There are no subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no account required. Just open the page and start generating words right away.
What categories are available?
▾
We offer 6 categories: Easy (simple everyday objects), Medium (moderate challenge), Hard (advanced vocabulary), Kids (child-friendly themes), Adults (grown-up topics), and Custom (you provide your own comma-separated word list). Click any category before generating.
Can I use this tool offline without internet?
▾
Yes! Once the page is fully loaded, the entire tool works offline. All word data is stored locally in the HTML file, so you can use it at home, in a car, on a plane — anywhere without Wi-Fi.
How do keyboard shortcuts work?
▾
Simply press the Spacebar to generate the next random word — no mouse click needed. This is great for fast-paced rounds where your hands are busy. Press Spacebar again and again to keep cycling through words.
How do I save favorite words?
▾
After generating a word you like, click the ★ (star) icon. It will appear in the Favorites section at the bottom of the tool. Favorites are saved in your browser's local storage, so they'll still be there next time you visit.
What is Shuffle Mode?
▾
When Shuffle Mode is ON, the generator ensures you won't see the same word twice in a single session. Once all words in a category have been shown, the list resets automatically. Enable it via the 🔀 button.
